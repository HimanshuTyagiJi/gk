Computer

A computer is an electronic device designed to process and store data. It is a versatile tool used in various fields of human knowledge and science, and has become an essential instrument for most tasks in today's world.

A computer operates using programs, which are sets of instructions written in human-readable language. These instructions can take various forms, such as numbers, text, images, etc.

The development of computers occurred when there was a need to perform calculations and carry out various tasks more efficiently. Early computers were very large and were used for specialized purposes in fields like science and mathematics.

However, nowadays, computers are used in all fields, including education, business, computer gaming, entertainment, and more. They come in various forms like personal computers, laptops, tablets, gaming computers, servers, etc.
